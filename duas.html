Duas & Supplications for Eid

تَقَبَّلَ اللّهُ مِنَّا وَمِنْكُمْ
Taqabbalallahu minna wa minkum
May Allah accept from us and from you
اللهم اجعلنا من عتقاء النار
Allahumma aj'alna min utaqā’i al-nār
O Allah, make us among those freed from the Fire
اللهم تقبل قرباننا
Allahumma taqabbal qurbanana
O Allah, accept our sacrifice
رَبَّنَا تَقَبَّلْ مِنَّا
Rabbana taqabbal minna
Our Lord! Accept from us
اللهم اجعل هذا العيد فرحة وسرورًا لنا وللمسلمين
Allahumma aj'al hadha al-eid farhatan wa sururan lana wa lil-muslimin
O Allah, make this Eid a joy and happiness for us and all Muslims
اللهم ارزقنا حج بيتك الحرام
Allahumma arzuqna hajja baytika al-haram
O Allah, grant us a pilgrimage to Your Sacred House
