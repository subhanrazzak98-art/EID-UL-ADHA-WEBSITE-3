About Eid al-Adha

Eid al-Adha commemorates the unwavering faith and obedience of Prophet Ibrahim (AS) who, in submission to Allah, was willing to sacrifice his son. Allah, in His mercy, replaced his son with a ram, signifying the value of devotion, sacrifice, and compassion. This blessed occasion is marked by prayers, sacrifice (qurbani), charity, and unity among Muslims worldwide.
