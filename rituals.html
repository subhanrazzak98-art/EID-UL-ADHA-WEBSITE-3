Rituals of Eid al-Adha

🕌

Eid Salah (Prayer)

Special congregational prayer held in mosques or open grounds. It begins with sermons and supplications.

🐑

Qurbani (Sacrifice)

Symbolic act of sacrificing a permitted animal, sharing its meat among family, friends, and the needy.

🕋

Hajj Pilgrimage

Pilgrims in Mecca perform rites of Hajj, including staying at Mina, standing at Arafah, and the symbolic stoning of the devil.

🌅

Fasting Arafah

Recommended for non-pilgrims on 9th Dhul-Hijjah, the day before Eid, as a means of spiritual purification.

💰

Charity (Sadaqah)

Essential to donate generously, distribute shares of sacrificial meat, and support the less fortunate.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Family & Community

Celebrations with loved ones, visiting elders, exchanging greetings, and spreading joy and unity.
